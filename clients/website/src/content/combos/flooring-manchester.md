## Manchester's single-level ranches are ideal for whole-home flooring

Because most of Manchester's housing — including the 55+ communities around Whiting such as **Crestwood Village, Leisure Village West, Leisure Knoll, and Cedar Glen Lakes** — is built on a single level, we can run one consistent flooring material through an entire ranch without navigating stairs or split levels. That makes whole-home flooring replacement more efficient here than in a typical two-story house.

## Low-threshold flooring for aging in place

The flooring decision that matters most in these communities isn't the color — it's the threshold. We install slip-resistant luxury vinyl plank with low-profile transitions between rooms, removing the small raised strips and height changes at doorways that are common trip hazards in original 1960s–80s construction. It's a straightforward change that makes a single-story home meaningfully safer to move through.

## Pine Lake Park and Renaissance homes

Newer construction near **Pine Lake Park** and at **Renaissance at Manchester** generally starts with more consistent subfloor conditions, so flooring projects there are more often about updating an existing material than correcting uneven transitions. We assess each home on its own before recommending a material or scope.
