## How does flooring hold up in a full Lakewood household?

It depends on the material. Lakewood's 2000s–2020s building boom filled **Westgate, Coventry Square, and Raintree** with duplexes and townhomes that regularly house larger families sharing common living spaces. That kind of daily traffic wears out carpet and can cup or scratch solid hardwood within a few years. We lead with **waterproof luxury vinyl plank** in these homes — it shrugs off scuffs, spills, and pet traffic while still giving you the look of wood or stone, and it holds up in kitchens and mudrooms where moisture is a daily reality, not an occasional risk.

## Single-level flooring for aging in place

In Leisure Village and Leisure Village East, flooring decisions are less about traffic volume and more about safety on a single level. We keep transitions flush between rooms, avoid thick thresholds that can catch a foot or a walker wheel, and steer toward low-pile or hard-surface materials that stay stable underfoot for years without becoming a maintenance project. It's a quieter kind of durability, built around how the home is actually used.
