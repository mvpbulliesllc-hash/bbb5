## The Fair Haven replacement spec

A roof replacement in Fair Haven, NJ typically runs **$9,500 to $20,000**, and Paragon builds every one of them to the same coastal spec: full tear-off to the deck, ice & water shield at eaves and valleys, synthetic underlayment, and **130 mph wind-rated architectural shingles fastened with six nails**. This is not a premium tier — it is the baseline a roof needs when it sits within a mile of a tidal, salt-laden river with an open-water fetch for northeast wind.

We tear off rather than lay over, every time. A second layer traps heat, hides deck rot, and voids most shingle warranties — and on Fair Haven's older stock, what's under the shingles is exactly what you need to see.

## What the tear-off usually reveals

Fair Haven packs three building eras into two square miles, and each one changes the job:

| Home type | Where | What we find at tear-off |
|---|---|---|
| Victorians & foursquares (1880s–1920s) | River Road, near the Dock | Plank decking with gaps, layered chimney and dormer flashing to rebuild |
| Capes & colonials (1920s–1950s) | Side streets off Ridge Road | Second- or third-cycle roofs, thin attic ventilation, ice-dam damage at eaves |
| Teardown-rebuild customs (1990s–today) | Borough-wide, thickest toward the river | Complex rooflines, designer-shingle color matching, first re-roofs now due |

On the pre-war homes especially, we inspect the deck board by board and replace anything soft or split **the same day** — with a photo — not on a change order two weeks later.

## The one-day process

1. **Protect** the beds, shrubs, and driveway; stage the dumpster off the grass.
2. **Tear off** to the deck and haul debris the same day.
3. **Inspect and repair** decking, then lay ice & water shield and underlayment.
4. **Install** wind-rated shingles, new flashing, drip edge, and ridge venting matched to your intake.
5. **Clean up** with a magnetic sweep of lawn and drive; register your warranty.

Most Fair Haven homes are finished between 7 AM and dinner. Bigger riverfront customs may run a second day.
