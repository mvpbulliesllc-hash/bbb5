Most Lakewood bathroom remodels run **$12,000–$40,000** — a hall-bath refresh with a tub-to-shower swap at the low end, a primary suite with a custom tiled shower and relocated plumbing at the top. Tile, waterproofing, and fixture relocation drive the number. Call 848-633-6440 for a free, itemized estimate broken out by the actual scope of your Lakewood bathroom.

## Two very different Lakewood households, two different priorities

In Leisure Village and Leisure Village East, the driving question is usually **aging in place**: converting a tub to a curbless or low-threshold shower, adding grab bars and a bench, and widening the doorway where the layout allows it. In Westgate, Coventry Square, and Raintree's duplexes and townhomes, the driving question is often **capacity** — a single shared bathroom serving a larger household, where adding a second full bath or upgrading a half-bath to full changes daily life more than any finish choice does.

## The waterproofing decision that matters most

Every Lakewood bathroom we remodel — whether it's a 1960s cape near the lakes or a 2015 townhome in Raintree — gets the same standard behind the tile: a proper waterproof membrane or backer system, a correctly sloped shower pan, and sealed penetrations. That discipline is what keeps a tub-to-shower conversion or a new tiled shower from becoming a hidden leak inside a wall five years later. It's the part of the job nobody sees, and the part we won't shortcut.

## Can you add a bathroom without adding square footage?

Often, yes. Basements and oversized primary suites are the two most common places Lakewood duplex and cape owners find room for an added bathroom — usually within the existing footprint by reworking a closet, hallway, or unused corner. As your general contractor, Paragon evaluates your existing plumbing chase, pulls the Lakewood Township permit, and coordinates the licensed plumbing and electrical the addition requires.
