## The symptoms we get called for in Manasquan

Roof repair in Manasquan usually starts with one of four calls: **shingles lifted or torn off in a nor easter, a ceiling stain that only shows in a hard blow, rusty streaks and leaks at chimney or skylight flashing, or granules washing out of the gutters on an aging in-town roof.** Most repairs run **$350–$1,500**, and for active leaks we can usually tarp the same day you call **848-633-6440**.

Each symptom points somewhere specific:

- **Missing or curled shingle edges** after a storm → uplift damage, the number-one Manasquan repair. Common on beach-block and Inlet homes that catch the fetch straight off the water.
- **Rust stains or drips near the chimney** → corroded flashing. The metal fails before the shingles here because salt attacks it from the windward side.
- **Stains only during driving rain** → a wind-driven flashing or ridge leak, not a hole. The water enters high and tracks down the deck.
- **Granules in the gutters, bald patches** → an old roof near end of life, common on the layered Victorians and foursquares by the train station.

## How we diagnose it

We inspect the whole windward slope, not just the wet spot — because on a salt-side roof the leak almost never enters where it drips. We check every flashing detail, the ridge, valleys, and pipe boots, and we document the roof with drone photography so you see the real condition, not a story. Then you get an itemized quote for the actual fix.
