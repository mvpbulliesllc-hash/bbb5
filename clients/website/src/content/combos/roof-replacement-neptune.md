A roof replacement in Neptune, NJ typically costs **$8,500 to $17,000** and, on most homes west of Route 18, is finished in a single day. Paragon Exteriors does a **full tear-off** to the deck — never a layover — then rebuilds with ice & water shield, synthetic underlayment, and 130 mph wind-rated architectural shingles fastened for the salt-and-wind punishment Neptune roofs actually take. ## How the day runs

1. **Tear-off** to bare deck, with same-day debris haul-away — no dumpster parked on your street for a week.
2. **Deck inspection** — soft or rotten plank/plywood swapped on the spot, common on the older Midtown two-stories.
3. **Waterproofing** — ice & water shield at eaves, valleys, and penetrations, run generously on low-slope bungalow porch roofs where wind-driven rain climbs uphill.
4. **Shingles & metal** — new drip edge, new chimney and wall flashing (we never reuse tired flashing), coastal-spec fastening.
5. **Ventilation** — ridge venting matched to your intake so summer attic heat doesn't cook the new shingles from below.
6. **Cleanup** — magnetic nail sweep of lawn and driveway, warranty registered for you, drone photos of the finished roof.
