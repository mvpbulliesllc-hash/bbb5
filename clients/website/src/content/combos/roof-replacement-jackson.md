## A roof built for Jackson's trees, not the beach

Paragon Exteriors replaces roofs across Jackson, NJ with a **full tear-off finished in one day** on most homes, at a typical cost of **$8,500–$17,000** for asphalt shingles. We file the construction permit with the Jackson Township Building Department, spec every roof for wooded-lot conditions, and hand you a free, itemized estimate before any work begins (NJ HIC #13VH13814500, fully insured).

Jackson roofs don't fight salt air — they fight the canopy. So our [roof replacement](/roof-replacement/) spec shifts inland: extra ice & water shield at the shaded eaves and valleys where winter snow refuses to melt, **algae-resistant architectural shingles** for north slopes under the oaks, and ventilation sized so summer attic heat doesn't cook the shingles from below. And because the open former-farmland stretches off Route 537 still take nor'easter gusts broadside, we keep six-nail high-wind fastening standard here too. Weighing shingle brands?
