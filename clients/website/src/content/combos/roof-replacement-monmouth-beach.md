## Built for salt on two sides

A full roof replacement in Monmouth Beach, NJ typically runs $9,000–$20,000 and, for most single-family homes, is torn off and re-shingled in a single day. Because this one-square-mile borough catches salt off the Atlantic and the Shrewsbury River at once, Paragon Exteriors builds every roof here to coastal spec: a complete tear-off, 130 mph wind-rated architectural shingles, six-nail fastening, and corrosion-resistant flashing and accessories. Estimates are free and itemized: 848-633-6440.

## The Monmouth Beach spec sheet

- **Full tear-off, never a layover** — down to the deck, so we see every soft spot the old roof was hiding
- **Deck inspection with plank-decking honesty** — the Gilded Age cottages near the oceanfront often have board decking under multiple shingle layers; we price any repair before work starts, not after
- **Ice & water shield past code minimums** at eaves, valleys, and penetrations — because wind-driven rain here will eventually find a way under a shingle
- **Synthetic underlayment** across the full deck, not felt
- **130 mph architectural shingles, six-nail high-wind fastening**, enhanced edge sealing on the Ocean Avenue side
- **Corrosion-resistant flashing, drip edge, and gutter hangers** — salt kills the metal long before the shingles
- **Borough permit and inspections handled** through the construction office on Beach Road
- **Magnetic nail sweep, same-day haul-away, and drone documentation** of the finished roof

## How the day runs

Tear-off starts early. We strip to the deck, replace any rotten plywood or plank on the spot, lay ice & water shield and synthetic underlayment, then install and fasten shingles to the high-wind spec. A magnetic sweep clears the lawn and driveway, the debris leaves the same day, and we fly a drone over the finished job so you can inspect every ridge and valley without climbing a ladder. On the Channel Club and river-side condos we coordinate the schedule directly with your board or property manager.
