## Diagnosis before dollar signs

Water almost never shows up on the ceiling directly below where it enters the roof. We start in the attic, trace the actual path, confirm it topside, and shoot drone photos of what we find — you're looking at your own flashing, not a stock picture and a scare pitch. On heavily treed lots off Allaire Road, those photos are often the first real look an owner has had at their own roof in years. If a storm caused the damage, our [storm damage](/storm-damage/) team documents everything to insurance standards before cleanup starts.
