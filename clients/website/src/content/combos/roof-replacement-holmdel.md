## A replacement built for Holmdel's rooflines

Most Holmdel roof replacements run **$10,000 to $22,000** and are finished in a single day, even on 3,000-plus square-foot customs. The wide range is roof geometry: Holmdel homes carry hips, dormers, dead valleys, and multiple chimneys, and every extra plane and transition adds flashing, labor, and shingle waste. Paragon Exteriors (NJ HIC #13VH13814500) measures the exact roof, itemizes it, and pulls the Holmdel Township permit as part of the job.

## The spec we install here

Holmdel isn't split-level tract housing — it's Village-core originals, 1960s–70s colonials near Crawfords Corner, and acre-lot customs from the 80s and 90s now on their first or second architectural roof. We spec to match:

- **Full tear-off to the deck** — never a layover buried over old shingles that hides rot
- **Deck inspection and repair** — soft plywood around chimneys and dead valleys swapped on the spot
- **Ice & water shield** at eaves, valleys, and every penetration — non-negotiable for these shaded, ice-dam-prone slopes
- **Synthetic underlayment** across the whole deck, not felt
- **Algae-resistant architectural shingles** with six-nail high-wind fastening for exposed Crawford Hill–area ridgelines
- **New step and counter flashing** at every dormer, wall, and chimney — the parts that actually failed
- **Balanced ridge venting** so summer attic heat stops cooking shingles from below

## How the day runs

Crew arrives early, tarps the landscaping, and tears the roof off in sections so it's never left open. We inspect and repair the deck, lay ice & water shield and underlayment, then shingle from the eaves up. A magnetic sweep clears every nail from lawn and driveway, and we [document the finished roof with drone photos](/projects/) so you see the valleys and flashing you'll never climb up to check yourself.
