## Zoning for Jackson's two-story colonials

A lot of the comfort complaints we hear in Jackson come from the same source: a single HVAC system trying to serve a full two-story colonial built during the township's 1990s–2000s growth wave. One thermostat can't keep both floors comfortable when the upstairs bakes in summer sun and the downstairs stays cool near the ductwork. Zoning solves that by letting each floor run to its own setpoint, and it's one of the most requested upgrades we install here.

## What size HVAC system does my Jackson home actually need?

System size comes from a load calculation — square footage, insulation, window count, ceiling height, and sun exposure — not a rule of thumb. Oversized systems short-cycle and leave humidity behind; undersized ones run constantly without keeping up. Between Cassville's older ranches and the township's larger colonials, home sizes vary enough in Jackson that guessing gets it wrong often. We measure and size to your actual home.
