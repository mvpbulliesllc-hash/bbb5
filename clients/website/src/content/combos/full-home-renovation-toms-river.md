## Renovations built for Toms River's housing stock

Toms River's housing runs from the 1960s–80s ranches and capes inland to waterfront homes along the Toms River and Barnegat Bay. Post-Sandy elevated rebuilds in Ortley Beach and Silverton are some of the most complex full renovations we take on, since flood-vent requirements, elevated framing, and utility relocation change the sequencing of the whole project. Inland, a dated Holiday City or North Dover ranch is more often a systems-and-finishes renovation — new plumbing, electrical, kitchen, and bathrooms behind the same walls.
