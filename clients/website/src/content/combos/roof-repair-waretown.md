## When your roof leaks tells us where it's failing

Waretown fights weather on two fronts, and that makes the *timing* of a leak the first diagnostic clue:

- **Leaks only during storms** point east. On Skippers Cove and Sands Point Harbor lagoon homes, a hard blow off the bay drives rain sideways under edge courses, ridge vents, and flashing that holds fine in normal rain. Homeowners chase these ghosts for years because the roof tests dry on a calm day.
- **Leaks in every decent rain** point west or to plain age: needle-dammed valleys and gutters on the wooded Brookville lots, split pipe boots and nail pops on 2000s Greenbriar Oceanaire ranches, and worn step flashing on the older capes near Waretown Lake.

Either way, we start in the attic, follow the water line back to its true entry point, and confirm it topside before naming a price — the stain on your ceiling is almost never under the hole.
