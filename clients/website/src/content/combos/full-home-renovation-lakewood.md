## How much does a full home renovation cost in Lakewood?

Whole-house renovations in Lakewood generally run **$75,000–$400,000+**, driven by square footage, how much of the work is structural versus cosmetic, and the finish level chosen. A sound home getting new systems and finishes throughout lands in the middle; a down-to-studs renovation with moved walls and premium finishes reaches the top. Call 848-633-6440 and we'll scope it in phases so you see exactly where the budget goes.

## Lakewood's oldest homes are prime renovation candidates

The lake district near **Lake Carasaljo and Georgian Court University** holds Lakewood's oldest housing stock — resort-era homes and 1950s–60s capes with original systems and layouts that have outlived their era but often sit on solid framing. That combination — good bones, dated everything else — is exactly what a full renovation is built for: new electrical, plumbing, and HVAC alongside a reworked kitchen and baths, without tearing the house down to get there.

## One contractor for the exterior and the interior

Most Lakewood contractors handle either the outside of a house or the inside — Paragon does both. On a full renovation that means the [roof](/roof-replacement/lakewood/), siding, and windows can be sequenced alongside the [kitchen](/kitchen-remodel/lakewood/), bathrooms, and flooring in a single managed project. ## Does Lakewood's newer housing ever need a full renovation too?

Yes. Duplexes and townhomes from Lakewood's 2000s–2020s building boom in **Westgate, Coventry Square, and Raintree** are now old enough that roofs, HVAC systems, and original builder-grade kitchens are aging out together, and several Leisure Village and Leisure Village East units still carry original 1960s–70s systems. When that many components need attention at once, bundling them into one phased renovation is usually more efficient than replacing each one separately over several years.
