## How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Manchester?

Most Manchester kitchen remodels run **$25,000–$90,000**. A cosmetic refresh of a compact 1960s–80s ranch kitchen in one of the 55+ villages around Whiting — new cabinets, counters, and appliances — sits toward the lower end; opening a wall or moving plumbing pushes the number higher. Call 848-633-6440 for a free, itemized estimate based on your actual kitchen, not a ballpark.

## Manchester's kitchens are mostly compact 55+ ranch layouts

Most of Manchester's housing is single-story, and a large share of it sits inside the 55+ communities around Whiting — **Crestwood Village, Leisure Village West, Leisure Knoll, and Cedar Glen Lakes** — built from the 1960s through the 1980s with original or first-generation kitchens sized for a different era of appliances and storage. The most common request we hear in these communities isn't a bigger kitchen; it's a smarter one that works better for the people cooking in it today.

## Getting more out of a ranch kitchen without expanding it

Because these are single-story homes, adding square footage usually means a costly addition. Instead, we focus on reworking what's already there: taller cabinets that use the full wall height, pull-out shelving in lower cabinets, wider counter runs, better lighting, and slip-resistant flooring underfoot that we coordinate directly with our [flooring](/flooring/manchester/) team. These changes add real day-to-day function to a ranch kitchen without touching the footprint or the roofline.

## Newer construction at Renaissance and Pine Lake Park

Not every Manchester kitchen needs a layout overhaul. Homes at **Renaissance at Manchester**, built in the 2000s, generally started with more generous kitchen footprints, so remodels there tend to focus on finishes — cabinet refacing, stone counters, updated appliances. Postwar capes and newer infill near **Pine Lake Park** fall somewhere in between, often needing both a finish upgrade and modest layout changes to open the kitchen toward the rest of the house.
