## Why does waterproofing matter more here than inland?

Brick's lagoon and bayfront neighborhoods — Shore Acres, Baywood, Midstreams, and toward Normandy Beach — carry more ambient humidity than a house set back from the water. That moisture works into bathroom walls and subfloor over years, and a bathroom that wasn't properly waterproofed often shows rot behind the tile before anything visible tells you. We install a full membrane system — not just caulk — under every pan and behind every wet wall, on every job.

## Aging-in-place bathrooms for Brick's 55+ homes

A big share of our bathroom work in Greenbriar and the older ranches around Herbertsville and Laurelton is aging-in-place: tub-to-shower conversions with curbless entries, comfort-height toilets, and grab bars built into the wall blocking rather than bolted on after the fact. It's the single highest-value remodel for a homeowner planning to stay put, and it typically fits the lower half of our price range since it doesn't require moving plumbing lines.
