## The Manasquan replacement spec

A roof replacement in Manasquan means a **full tear-off down to the deck and a rebuild sized for open-ocean exposure** — 130 mph wind-rated architectural shingles, six-nail fastening, sealed starter courses, and corrosion-resistant edge metal, with the Borough permit and inspections handled by us. Most homes are done in a single day, and typical projects run **$8,500–$18,000** depending on size, pitch, buried layers, and how close you sit to the water.

Every roof gets the same build regardless of which part of the borough it is on:

| Layer | What goes down | Why it matters in Manasquan |
|---|---|---|

## How a one-day install runs

We stage the driveway early, tear the old roof off the beach-block or in-town home in a morning, and inspect the deck before anything covers it. Ice & water shield and synthetic underlayment go down, then the shingles, ridge venting matched to your attic intake, and new flashing at every chimney, wall, and pipe — we never reuse tired flashing on a salt-side home. We finish with a magnetic nail sweep of the lawn and driveway and haul the debris the same day, so no dumpster sits on your block for a week. We document the whole job with drone photography, so you see exactly what came off and what went on.
