## How much does HVAC installation cost in Manchester?

Most Manchester HVAC installations run **$6,000–$14,000**, depending on system type, home size, and existing ductwork. A right-sized heat pump for a compact single-story 55+ ranch typically falls toward the lower end of that range. Call 848-633-6440 for a free, itemized estimate based on a proper load calculation for your home, not a rule-of-thumb number.

## Right-sizing matters more in Manchester's ranches than the brand does

Much of Manchester's housing stock — including the 55+ communities around Whiting such as **Crestwood Village, Leisure Village West, Leisure Knoll, and Cedar Glen Lakes** — consists of compact single-story ranches built in the 1960s through the 1980s. It's common to find an original system oversized for the home's actual square footage, which wastes energy and shortens the equipment's life. We run a proper load calculation for the specific home rather than simply matching the old unit's tonnage.

## Efficient heat pumps for single-level homes

A single-story ranch is a good candidate for a modern SEER2-rated heat pump, which heats and cools from one efficient outdoor unit instead of running a furnace and separate central air system side by side. That's a meaningful upgrade for the many Manchester homes where both systems are original and aging out together.

## Newer homes at Renaissance still need the right-sized system

Homes at **Renaissance at Manchester** are newer, but "newer" doesn't always mean "right-sized" — builder-installed systems are sized to a standard formula, not to the specific home. We size every replacement to the actual house, whether it's a 2000s build at Renaissance or a 1960s ranch in Whiting.
