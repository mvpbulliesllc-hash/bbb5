## How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Point Pleasant?

Most Point Pleasant kitchen remodels land between $25,000 and $90,000, depending on scope. A cabinet and counter refresh on an existing layout sits at the low end; a full gut that moves plumbing, reworks electrical, and adds custom cabinetry runs toward the top. We walk your kitchen in person and hand you a free, itemized estimate before any demo starts, so the price you're quoted is the price you pay.

## One general contractor, every trade

Point Pleasant's housing stock is a mix of eras. Post-war capes, ranches, and split-levels from the 1950s through 70s fill West Point Pleasant and Lovelandtown, most with closed-off galley kitchens that were never built for how families cook and gather today. Along Beaver Dam Creek, the canal, and out toward Bay Head Shores, waterfront and lagoon homes often carry different concerns — humidity, salt air, and in some cases flood-zone construction that affects where cabinetry and outlets can sit. Either way, as your general contractor we manage the plumber, electrician, and any gas line work under one roof, so you're not juggling three separate contractors.

## What's included in a Paragon kitchen remodel

- **Layout planning** — opening walls, relocating the sink or range, widening galley footprints common in 1950s–70s capes
- **Cabinetry and counters** — semi-custom or custom cabinets, quartz, granite, or butcher block, with moisture-resistant options for homes near Beaver Dam Creek or the canal
- **Plumbing and electrical** — new supply lines, disposal hookups, and panel updates where older wiring can't support modern appliances
- **Permits and inspections** — pulled through Point Pleasant Borough and scheduled around your timeline

## Do I need permits for a kitchen remodel in Point Pleasant?

Most kitchen remodels touch plumbing, electrical, or both, and Point Pleasant Borough requires permits any time those systems change — a moved sink, a new gas line, relocated outlets. (Point Pleasant Beach is a separate municipality with its own department, so we confirm which office applies before filing.) We pull every permit ourselves and coordinate the inspections, so you're not stuck tracking down paperwork for three different trades.
