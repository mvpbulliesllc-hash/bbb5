## How much does HVAC installation cost in Lakewood?

Most central-air or heat-pump replacements in Lakewood run **$6,000–$14,000 installed**, set by system size, SEER2 efficiency, ductwork condition, and whether the furnace or air handler is replaced alongside the condenser. Call 848-633-6440 for a free, itemized estimate sized to your actual home — not a one-size number pulled from square footage alone.

## How do you size HVAC for a Lakewood ranch-style 55+ home?

With a load calculation, not a guess. Leisure Village and Leisure Village East are built almost entirely of single-story ranch homes, many still running original ductwork and equipment from the 1960s and 70s. Replacing that equipment isn't a straight swap — we run a load calculation based on the home's actual insulation, window count, and layout, because decades-old systems in these communities were often sized by rule of thumb rather than measurement. The right-sized replacement runs quieter, costs less to operate, and manages humidity better than an oversized unit ever will.

## Duplex owners: one mobilization, two systems

Lakewood's building-boom sections — **Westgate, Coventry Square, Raintree** — are full of duplexes and townhomes built with matching HVAC equipment on both sides, and that equipment tends to reach the end of its service life on both sides around the same time. When one owner is already replacing a failing system, it's worth checking in with the other side: scheduling both installs together saves on crew mobilization and equipment coordination versus running the same job twice.
