## Specced for the Pine Barrens, not the beach

Manchester roofs die from trees, heat, and ice — not salt spray — so our [roof replacement](/roof-replacement/) spec shifts accordingly. **Algae-resistant architectural shingles** keep pine-shaded slopes from streaking and mossing over. Ice & water shield runs generously along the long ranch eaves where winter dams form. Every system carries a **Class A fire rating** — non-negotiable in a township that watched a wildfire burn off Route 539 in 2023 — and ridge venting is sized for attics that bake past 130°F on sandy, full-sun lots. Decking softened under years of needle-packed valleys gets swapped on the spot, never shingled over.

## Install day, village edition

The paperwork is finished before the crew ever pulls up: permit approved, association color signed off. From there it's one contained day — arrival around 7 AM, driveway and plantings protected, tear-off through finished ridge cap by late afternoon, debris hauled the same day so no dumpster spends a week in a community where everybody walks. We close with a magnetic nail sweep of the lawn and sidewalks, and the drone photos we shoot of every project mean you — and your association — can inspect the finished roof from a lawn chair.
