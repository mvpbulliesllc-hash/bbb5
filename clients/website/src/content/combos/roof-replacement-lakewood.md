## The spec follows the decade your block was built

Lakewood spans more building eras than any town in Ocean County, so a [roof replacement](/roof-replacement/) here isn't one job — it's four:

| Where you live | What the replacement actually involves |
|---|---|
| Resort-era homes near the lakes and Georgian Court | Steep pitches, real chimney reflashing, framing that predates plywood — deck allowance quoted up front, not discovered mid-job |
| Postwar capes and ranches toward County Line Road | Often a third shingle cycle; tired boards replaced, ice & water shield run deep at the eaves where these attics build ice dams |
| Leisure Village, Leisure Village East, Fairways at Lake Ridge | Low-slope ranch roofs — and in several villages the association owns the roof, so we confirm ownership and approvals first |
| Boom-era duplexes and townhomes in Westgate, Coventry Square, Raintree | Builder-grade shingles aging out on schedule; we upgrade to heavier architectural shingles and keep shared ridgelines continuous |

On the wooded lots, algae-resistant shingles are our default — shaded north slopes here streak black long before they're actually worn.
