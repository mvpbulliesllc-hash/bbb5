## What does interior door installation cost in New Jersey?

Installed interior doors in Ocean and Monmouth County typically run **$350–$1,200 each**, depending on whether it's a hollow-core builder door, a solid-core upgrade, a barn-door setup, or a custom size that needs the opening reframed. New casing and hardware add to it, and doing several at once lowers the per-door cost. Your estimate is itemized per opening.

## The detail that makes a house feel finished

Doors are the trim-carpentry detail people feel before they notice — a door that binds, a gap at the top, casing with a bad miter. Hung right, doors close with a soft click, swing true, and make a whole hallway look intentional. It's precise work: **plumb the jamb, shim it true, set consistent reveals, and miter the casing tight.** That's the standard we hold.

## What a Paragon interior door job includes

- **Assessment of each opening** — new slab on an existing jamb where it's square, pre-hung where it isn't
- **Hollow-core, solid-core, barn, bypass, bifold, or pocket** doors to fit the room and budget
- **Solid blocking** behind barn-door and pocket-door hardware — never anchored to drywall alone
- **Plumb, shimmed, and reveal-set** so every door swings and latches true
- **New casing and trim** mitered clean, caulked, and ready for paint
- **Hardware** — hinges, knobs or levers, and stops installed and aligned
- **Old-door haul-away** and cleanup
