## How much does a full home renovation cost in New Jersey?

Whole-house renovations in Ocean and Monmouth County generally run **$75,000–$400,000+**, set by square footage, how much is structural versus cosmetic, whether kitchens and baths are gutted, and the finish level. A sound home getting new finishes throughout sits lower; a down-to-studs renovation with moved walls, new systems, and premium finishes reaches the top. We scope it in phases so the number is transparent, not a mystery.

- **One contract and one point of contact** from demolition to final punch list
