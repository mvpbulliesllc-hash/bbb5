## What does exterior house painting cost in New Jersey?

Most exterior repaints in Ocean and Monmouth County run **$4,500–$12,000**, set by home size, stories, siding material, and — more than anything — how much prep and repair the surface needs. A quote that looks cheap usually skipped the prep. Ours is itemized so you can see exactly what the surface work costs versus the coating.

## At the shore, prep is the whole job

Salt air and relentless sun punish exterior coatings, and they find every shortcut. We **scrape, sand, and spot-prime bare wood, caulk failed seams, and treat any mildew** before a finish coat ever goes on. On homes near the water we spec coatings rated for coastal exposure. That's the difference between a repaint that still looks sharp in eight years and one that's peeling in two.

## What every Paragon exterior paint job includes

- **Pressure or soft wash** to strip chalk, salt, and mildew so paint can bond
- **Scrape, sand, and spot-prime** all bare and failing areas
- **Minor carpentry and caulking** of failed seams and trim before coating
- **Premium exterior coatings** matched to your substrate (wood, fiber cement, or vinyl-safe)
- **Trim, doors, and shutters** cut in clean by hand
- **Surfaces and landscaping protected**, then a full site cleanup
- **Two coats where the surface calls for it** — not one thin pass
