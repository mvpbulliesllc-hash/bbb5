## How much does new flooring cost in New Jersey?

Installed flooring across Ocean and Monmouth County typically runs **$4–$14 per square foot** — luxury vinyl plank at the low end, engineered and solid hardwood in the middle, porcelain tile at the top once subfloor prep and setting are counted. The right number depends on the material, the condition of what's underneath, and how many transitions and stairs the job includes. Your free estimate itemizes it room by room.

## Coastal homes need moisture-smart floors

Solid hardwood is beautiful, but a humid bayfront or barrier-island home can cup and gap it within a season. That's why we lead with **waterproof luxury vinyl plank** near the water and reserve solid hardwood for drier, elevated interiors. Matching the floor to the room's real moisture risk is the difference between a floor that looks new in ten years and one you replace in three.
