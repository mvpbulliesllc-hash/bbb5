## What does HVAC installation cost in New Jersey?

Most central-air or heat-pump replacements in Ocean and Monmouth County run **$6,000–$14,000 installed**, set by system size, SEER2 efficiency, ductwork condition, and whether the furnace or air handler is replaced at the same time. Comfort at the shore is not optional — humid bay summers and cold, wind-driven winters both push a system hard — so we size and install for the climate your home actually lives in.

## Comfort is part of the whole-home picture

A new roof, tight windows, and sealed siding all change how your HVAC performs — a well-sealed home needs less tonnage, not more. Because Paragon works across your whole exterior, we look at the envelope and the equipment together instead of overselling a bigger unit to cover for air leaks. That is how you get lower bills, not just new gear.

## What every Paragon HVAC install includes

- **Manual-J-style load calculation** — the system is sized to your home, not guessed from square footage
- **New condenser or heat pump** matched to a compatible air handler or furnace
- **Ductwork inspection** with sealing or repair where it is losing conditioned air
- **New line set, pad, and electrical whip** — we do not reuse tired components to save an hour
- **Smart or programmable thermostat** set up before we leave
- **Refrigerant charge verified** to spec and airflow balanced room to room
- **Permit and inspection** handled where your town requires them
- **Full cleanup** and old-equipment haul-away
