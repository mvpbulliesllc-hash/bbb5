## How much does a kitchen remodel cost in New Jersey?

Kitchen remodels in Ocean and Monmouth County generally run **$25,000–$90,000+** — a cosmetic refresh at the low end, a full gut with a new layout, relocated utilities, custom cabinetry, and premium appliances at the top. Cabinets, countertops, and whether walls or plumbing move are the levers that set your number. You get an itemized estimate by scope, not a vague "kitchens start at."

## One general contractor, every trade managed

A kitchen touches carpentry, plumbing, electrical, tile, counters, and sometimes structure. The projects that go sideways are the ones where a homeowner is juggling five subs. - **Design and layout** — from a straight replacement to opening the kitchen up and moving the work triangle

- **Licensed plumbing, electrical, and gas** rough-in and finish, with permits pulled by us
- **Cabinetry** — stock, semi-custom, or custom, ordered early to protect the schedule
- **Countertops** — quartz, granite, or butcher block, templated and installed
