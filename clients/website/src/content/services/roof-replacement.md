## The one-day roof replacement, done right

A roof replacement is the single most important weather-defense investment your home will ever get — and the place where corner-cutting hides best. At Paragon Exteriors we replace roofs the way we'd do our own: **full tear-off** (never a layover unless you specifically want one and code allows it), a real deck inspection with rotten plywood replaced on the spot, ice & water shield at eaves and valleys, synthetic underlayment, and architectural shingles fastened to the manufacturer's high-wind spec.

That's how we kept quality intact even during our famous **30-roofs-in-30-days** run — the process doesn't change, only the schedule does.

## What's included in every Paragon roof replacement

- **Complete tear-off** of existing shingles and underlayment, down to the deck
- **Deck inspection & repair** — damaged plywood swapped before anything goes over it
- **Ice & water shield** at eaves, valleys, and penetrations (NJ winters demand it)
- **Synthetic underlayment** across the full deck — not felt paper
- **Architectural shingles** installed with six-nail high-wind fastening
- **New flashing** at chimneys, walls, and pipes — we don't reuse tired flashing
- **Ridge venting** matched to your attic's intake so the shingles last their full life
- **Magnetic nail sweep + same-day debris haul-away** — your lawn like we were never there

## Built for Jersey Shore weather

Ocean and Monmouth County roofs live a harder life than most: salt air, nor'easters, 60+ mph gusts off the water, summer heat, and freeze-thaw winters. We spec every roof for the exposure it will actually face — higher wind-rated fastening near the water, upgraded edge metal on bayfront homes, and ventilation done right so summer attic heat doesn't cook your shingles from below.
